About Our School

Our History

Shree Secondary School Imiliya was established in 2032 with a vision to provide quality education to the students of our community. Over the past decades, we have grown from a small institution to one of the most respected schools in the region.

We are committed to academic excellence while fostering creativity, critical thinking, and character development. Our dedicated team of over 80 qualified teachers works tirelessly to ensure each of our 2300+ students receives personalized attention and guidance.

हाम्रो विद्यालयले शैक्षिक उपलब्धि मात्र होइन, जीवनका लागि तयार गरिने पूर्ण व्यक्तित्वको विकासमा विश्वास गर्छ।

"हामी विश्वास गर्छौं कि शिक्षा केवल शैक्षिक सफलता मात्र होइन; व्यक्तित्व विकास, सामाजिक उत्तरदायित्व र रचनात्मक क्षमताको विकास पनि हो।"

Located at Buddha bhumi 9 Imiliya our campus provides a conducive learning environment with modern facilities, dedicated staff, and a curriculum designed to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Rules & Regulations

Guidelines to ensure a safe and productive learning environment

  • Minimum 75% attendance required for all students
  • Regular punctuality is mandatory
  • Medical certificates required for extended absences
  • Parents will be notified of irregular attendance
  • School uniform must be worn daily
  • Neat and tidy appearance expected at all times
  • ID cards must be visible
  • Proper footwear required
  • Respectful behavior towards teachers and peers
  • Mobile phones must be switched off during class
  • No bullying or harassment tolerated
  • Campus premises must be kept clean
  • Strict prohibition of drugs, tobacco, and alcohol
  • All assignments must be original work
  • Cheating and plagiarism are strictly prohibited
  • Regular homework and project completion required
  • Proper citation of sources mandatory
  • Visitors must register at the main office
  • Students must not leave campus without permission
  • Emergency procedures must be followed
  • Report any safety concerns immediately
